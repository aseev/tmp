As part of our commitment to maintaining a productive and disturbance-free work environment, we wish to remind everyone of the firm's policy regarding the management of device sounds. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

Personal Device Notifications: Ensure that all notifications on personal devices are set to mute to avoid disturbing your colleagues.

Notification Management: Confirm that all notifications are directed through your headphones, ensuring no sound is audible from your devices.

Handling Personal Calls: If you need to take a personal call, please speak in a subdued voice or relocate to an area where you will not disrupt others.

Adhering to these guidelines helps promote a respectful and focused work atmosphere for all.
